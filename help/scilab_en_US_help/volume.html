volume

volume computes the specific volume of humid air given the dry bulb temperature, the humidity and the total pressure

Syntax

[v]=volume(Tdry,W,p)

Parameters

Tdry:

dry bulb temperature (in K)

W:

humidity (in kg/kg of dry air)

p:

total pressure (in Pa)

v:

specific volume (in cu. m/kg of dry air)

Description

volume computes the specific volume v (in cu. m/kg of dry air) of humid air given the dry bulb temperature Tdry (in K), the humidity W (in kg/kg of dry air) and the total pressure p (in Pa). By default, total pressure is assumed to be the atmospheric pressure at sea level (p = 101325). volume is a main function of the psychrometrics toolbox for Scilab.

Examples

// Compute the specific volume given
// the dry bulb temperature is 25 °C and
// the humidity is 7 g/kg of dry air
// at 1 atm total pressure:
Tdry=25+273.15; // dry bulb temperature in K
W=7e-3; // humidity in kg/kg of dry air
v=volume(Tdry,W) // specific volume in cu. m/kg of dry air
